UDSM & CRDB FinHub Incubator

Team Member

2020 - Present

University of Dar-es-Salaam (UDSM), Tanzania

The UDSM & CRDB Fin Hub Incubator is a challenge-driven innovation space that provides a platform for young people to develop skills and solve challenges in the financial sector. The TCMS project, run in collaboration with CRDB Bank, identifies talents, provides mentorship, and builds capacity to address fintech challenges in CRDB Bank and other partners. The project creates a pipeline of talent that can be onboarded to the financial sector, contributing to its growth and development in Tanzania and beyond. As a team, we developed a working prototype of an innovative idea that aims at reducing customer service time at the bank through the use of Electronic Cheques (E-Cheque). Read more about FINHUB.
