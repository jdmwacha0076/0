Smart Girls in ICT

Trainer and Facilitator

2023 - Present

Dar-es-Salaam, Tanzania

As a trainer at Feza Girls Secondary School, I am excited to introduce the "Smart Girls in ICT" program initiated by the International Telecommunication Union (ITU). This program is specifically designed to encourage young girls and women to participate in the information and communication technology (ICT) industry. Through the program, we provide training, mentorship, and networking opportunities to our students so as to bridge the gender gap in the ICT sector. Our goal is to inspire and encourage girls to pursue careers in ICT and provide them with the Human Centered Design skills and knowledge necessary to succeed in this field. Read more.

UDSM & CRDB FinHub Incubator

Team Member

2020 - Present

University of Dar-es-Salaam (UDSM), Tanzania

The UDSM & CRDB Fin Hub Incubator is a challenge-driven innovation space that provides a platform for young people to develop skills and solve challenges in the financial sector. The TCMS project, run in collaboration with CRDB Bank, identifies talents, provides mentorship, and builds capacity to address fintech challenges in CRDB Bank and other partners. The project creates a pipeline of talent that can be onboarded to the financial sector, contributing to its growth and development in Tanzania and beyond. As a team, we developed a working prototype of an innovative idea that aims at reducing customer service time at the bank through the use of Electronic Cheques (E-Cheque). Read more.
